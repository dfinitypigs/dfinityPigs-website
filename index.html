OUR STORY

The wolf chased the pigs to their house and tried to blow it away with his strong breath. The three pigs trembled with fear and looked for a way out of the mess created by the evil beast. One of them saw a book behind the fireplace, a book he had never seen before. Inside the books pages he discovered a magical portal to the multivers.

The portal engulfed the pigs and beamed them to the World of DfinityPigs. But they found themselves in a dark world, ruled by ten evil wolves. They have decided to hide and analyze this new world. They discovered that the pigs living on this planet were inslaved by the wolves. They wore shabby clothes and were very unhappy.

The three pigs have decided to do something against the overlords. Using rovers to race around the planet, they have managed to rescue their enslaved siblings one by one, and start a rebellion. They drove the wolves from the throne and locked them into a prison, where they can no longer harm anyone. Peace was restored to the world.
